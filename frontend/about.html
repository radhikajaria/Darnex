About Rail Traffic Control

Rail Traffic Control is a centralized system that monitors and manages train movements efficiently. It ensures safety, optimal track usage, and timely operations across complex railway networks.

The system uses AI-powered recommendations to assist train traffic controllers in making quick and effective decisions, such as rerouting trains, delaying departures, and managing congestion.

By leveraging advanced analytics and real-time monitoring, this platform minimizes delays, reduces risks, and ensures a smooth railway operation.
